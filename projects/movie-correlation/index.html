Movie Correlation with Python

What I was curious about

I wanted to test which movie features really move gross revenue and which factors are often overstated when people talk about blockbuster performance.

What I did

I cleaned and typed the movie dataset in Python, built a numeric correlation matrix, and compared strength of relationships across budget, votes, score, and gross.

Why it matters

  • Shows practical exploratory analysis in Python with clear hypothesis testing.
  • Separates intuition from evidence using measurable correlation signals.
  • Demonstrates reproducible notebook workflow for data cleaning and analysis.
