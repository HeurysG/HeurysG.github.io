League of Legends Match Outcome

End-to-end modeling of League of Legends match outcomes using player and team objectives. Covers exploratory data analysis, Random Forests, Gradient Boosting (GBM), cross-validation, and feature importance to explain what drives wins.

Highlights

  • 97% match outcome accuracy via GBM; Random Forest close at 96%.
  • Turret and inhibitor control are the strongest predictors of victory.
  • Feature importance and cross-validation guard against overfitting.
