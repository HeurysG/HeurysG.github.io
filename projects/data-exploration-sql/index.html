Data Exploration in SQL

What I was curious about

I wanted to understand how COVID-19 cases, deaths, and vaccinations changed across countries and over time, and turn that into clean business-style trends and comparisons.

What I did

I used SQL joins, rolling window calculations, CTEs, and temporary views to compare infection rates, death percentages, and vaccination progress across locations and dates.

Why it matters

  • Shows practical SQL analysis on a multi-table, time-series dataset.
  • Demonstrates KPI thinking by translating raw fields into interpretable metrics.
  • Builds a reusable pattern for trend, ratio, and cohort-style analysis.
