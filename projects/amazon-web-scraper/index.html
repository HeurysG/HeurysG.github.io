Amazon Web Scraper with Python

What I was curious about

I wanted a lightweight way to monitor product pricing over time and trigger a simple workflow when a tracked product moves into a target range.

What I did

I built a Python scraper that pulls product details, captures price snapshots, appends them to a local dataset, and supports repeatable checks for ongoing monitoring.

Why it matters

  • Demonstrates practical automation from raw webpage data to trackable signals.
  • Shows basic pipeline thinking: extract, append, and monitor over time.
  • Useful foundation for lightweight pricing alerts and trend analysis.
